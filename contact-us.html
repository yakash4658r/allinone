Common Questions

How quickly can you scope a project?
We typically deliver a scoped engagement document within 48 business hours of your initial enquiry. This includes a high-level architecture, timeline estimate, and team composition.
What is the minimum engagement size?
We work with projects starting from a 12-week MVP engagement. We don't take on one-off tasks or hourly consulting — we partner for outcomes, not hours.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Yes. We work with founders, product companies, and enterprises. If you're an early-stage founder with a clear problem, we want to hear from you.
Which time zones do you support?
Our teams run on timezone overlap with US (EST/PST), UK (GMT), and ANZ clients. Shared sprint rituals, not just async handoffs.
